Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I download previous year question papers?

To download previous year question papers, navigate to the PYQ Downloader tool on our website. Select your university/board, course, subject, and year from the dropdown menus. Click on the search button to find relevant papers, and then click the download button to save the PDF to your device.

Are all the study materials free to access?

We offer both free and premium study materials. Basic study materials are available to all users, while comprehensive and specialized materials are available to our Pro and Premium subscribers. Check our pricing plans for more details on what's included in each subscription tier.

How often are new study materials added?

We update our study materials regularly, typically adding new content weekly. We prioritize adding materials for upcoming exams and frequently requested topics. If you need specific materials that aren't available, you can request them through our feedback form.

Can I contribute my own study materials or notes?

Yes, we welcome contributions from students and educators! If you have high-quality notes or study materials you'd like to share, please use the 'Contribute' option in the Study Materials section. Our team will review your submission and, if approved, make it available to other users with proper attribution.

How do I cancel my premium subscription?

To cancel your premium subscription, log in to your account, go to 'Account Settings', and select 'Subscription'. Click on 'Cancel Subscription' and follow the prompts. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period, after which it will not renew.
