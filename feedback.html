Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our feedback process

How long does it take to process feedback?

We typically review and process all feedback within 2-3 business days. For urgent issues or critical bug reports, we prioritize them and aim to respond within 24 hours.

Will I receive a response to my feedback?

Yes, we respond to all feedback submissions. You will receive an acknowledgment email immediately after submission, followed by a more detailed response once your feedback has been reviewed by our team.

How are feature requests evaluated?

Feature requests are evaluated based on several factors including user demand, technical feasibility, alignment with our platform goals, and potential impact on user experience. We maintain a feature request backlog and prioritize them for future development cycles.

Can I track the status of my feedback or request?

Yes, you can track the status of your feedback or request by logging into your QUARYNOX account and visiting the "My Feedback" section. If you submitted feedback without an account, you can use the reference number provided in your acknowledgment email to check the status.

How can I provide additional information for my submitted feedback?

If you need to provide additional information for your submitted feedback, you can reply to the acknowledgment email you received or use the reference number to update your submission through our feedback portal.
